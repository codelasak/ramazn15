Pano15 is a private utility app for students of a single Turkish religious vocational high school (Bahçelievler 15 Temmuz Şehitleri Anadolu İmam Hatip Lisesi) in Istanbul.

WHY ACCOUNT IS REQUIRED:
The core value (mock-exam tracking, personalized study schedule, profile, prayer notifications) is tied to a student account. Demo credentials provided.

DEMO ACCOUNT (student role, class 12A, day-student):
Username: reviewer@pano15.test
Password: Pano15Reviewer2026!

HOW TO REVIEW (5 minutes):
1. Launch app — splash → login screen
2. Login with demo credentials
3. Bottom nav, tap each tab:
   - Ana Sayfa: dashboard with prayer-time countdown, today's classes, study sessions
   - Program: full week class schedule for 12A (Mon-Fri tabs)
   - Takip: mock-exam tracker, tap + to add a sample result
   - Ibadet: prayer times + Quran reader (tap "Dinle" on a verse for audio)
   - Profil: profile info; "Hesabimi Sil" button at bottom satisfies Guideline 5.1.1(v)

ACCOUNT DELETION:
Profile tab > "Hesabimi Sil" > type "SIL" to confirm > account + all data permanently deleted.

THIRD-PARTY APIs (read-only, no PII sent):
- diyanet.gov.tr (Turkish official prayer times)
- api.alquran.cloud (Quran text + audio recitations)
- nominatim.openstreetmap.org (district lookup)

DEFAULT LOCATION: Istanbul (district 9541), target audience is Istanbul-based.
